Cloudseeker Mission

In 2027, Nephelis Industries will launch the Cloudseeker miniprobe to Venus, riding a shared SpaceX rocket. A balloon probe will float in the planet’s atmosphere for 30 days, capturing imagery and data on composition, winds, and potential biosignatures. Relayed via a CubeSat orbiter, this data will fuel humanity’s dream of Venusian exploration—all for under $500,000, powered by volunteers and donations.

The Cloudseeker probe is a 1-meter balloon with a lightweight sensor pod (camera, temperature, pressure) and a 3U CubeSat orbiter for data relay. Our goal: prove low-cost, high-impact missions can unlock the cosmos. Venus’s clouds hold secrets—let’s uncover them together.

Flight4Science

Flight4Science is Nephelis Industries’ non-profit platform, democratizing space by sharing payload capacity on commercial rockets. From student experiments to micro-missions like Cloudseeker, we make space accessible. Partner with us to launch your vision.
