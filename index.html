The Mission

Nephelis Industries presents Project AETHER, a landmark mission to Venus. Our miniprobe, Cloudseeker, will lay the foundation for humanity’s next great leap by unlocking the immense energy and resource potential of the Venusian atmosphere.

Launching in 2027, Cloudseeker targets the 50-55 km altitude band, where temperatures and pressure mirror Earth-like conditions, to validate models for habitation, resource extraction, and astrobiological discovery.

Technology

Cloudseeker thrives in Venus’s harsh environment by leveraging a stack of miniaturized, resilient technologies designed for maximum efficiency and durability.

  • Acid-Resistant Aerostat: A fluoropolymer balloon designed to withstand sulfuric acid clouds and extreme atmospheric pressures.
  • Integrated Sensor Suite: A compact, 1kg pod containing a 4K camera, mass spectrometers, and environmental sensors.
  • 3U CubeSat Relay: A dedicated CubeSat in orbit ensures high-bandwidth data transmission back to Earth.
